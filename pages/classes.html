Safety Information

Please make sure that you follow all the safety instructions at all times. If you have any questions, please talk to a member of staff.

Bouldering Safely

  • REMOVE JEWELLERY BEFORE CLIMBING
  • NO HARD OBJECTS OR FOOD/DRINK ON THE MATTING
  • NO LYING ON THE MATTING
  • WARM UP
  • NO RUNNING
  • DO NOT WALK/ STAND IN LANDING AREA OF FELLOW CLIMBERS
  • DO NOT JUMP OFF ALWAYS CLIMB DOWN
  • DO NOT RELY ON MATTING TO ABSORB IMPACT WHEN FALLING
  • REPORT ANY LOOSE OR SPINNING HOLDS TO RECEPTION

Climber Responsibility

You should be in good health and participate responsibly. If you have any concerns about any of the equipment or another climbers behaviour, please bring it to the attention of a member of staff. Please do not climb if you think this activity might put your health at risk, or aggravate a pre-existing condition in any way. Climbing is not recommended for guests that have neck or back problems, heart problems or have had recent surgery. If you are pregnant you should consider your physical condition and limitations before deciding to participate. If at any time you do not feel well please speak to a member of staff.
